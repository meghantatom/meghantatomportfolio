The Howl

The Howl was attended by 5500 students. I brought food trucks to it for the first time ever and worked hard to bring new innovation. I raised the tickets prices to raise more money for the rest of the year. I added doors to the field house so we could put more people in the dance. 4,400 people were in the field house at once. Year's prior only 2,200 people could fit in the fieldhouse so I am so glad I spent the money on that.
